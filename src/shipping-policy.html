Direct From Manufacturer

To ensure you get professional-grade equipment at the best possible prices, we ship your gear directly from our specialized manufacturing facility in Kishnagarh, Chandigarh. This direct-to-consumer model allows us to cut out middleman markups and ensure every bat and jersey meets our strict quality control standards before it leaves the workshop.

Customs & Duties

For customers in the USA, orders under $800 are generally exempt from import duties. We handle all export documentation. In the rare event of custom delays, we will notify you immediately.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package end-to-end, from our facility in Chandigarh to your doorstep in the US.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. For size exchanges, please contact our support team. Note that items must be unused and in original packaging.
